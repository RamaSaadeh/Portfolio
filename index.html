I've been working in Dubai across product and tech roles while finishing my degree. I'm drawn to early-stage products where things are still being figured out: real constraints, decisions that matter, messy briefs.

01

Beit La Beit

Final Year Project — Loughborough University, 2025

Product Design Framer UX Research Figma

Designed and prototyped a delivery booking and payment platform for homemade micro-businesses in Dubai — a gap no existing product properly addresses.

Conducted primary research across three stakeholder groups: business owners, delivery executives, and customers. Built a high-fidelity bilingual (English/Arabic) interactive prototype in Framer, mapping 22 functional requirements from research through to tested prototype.

Scored above DHL, Aramex, and Fetchr on cost-of-ownership. Closest competitor in functionality to DHL at a fraction of the infrastructure overhead.
